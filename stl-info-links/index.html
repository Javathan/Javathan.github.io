STL Information

St. Theresa Of Lisieux Catholic High School is a top-ranking high school in Canada, known for its rigorous academic programs and commitment to student success. The school offers a wide range of opportunities including Advanced Placement (AP) courses, Specialist High Skills Majors (SHSM), and Co-operative Education (Co-Op) programs to help students excel in both academics and the workforce. With a vast array of clubs, sports teams, and extracurricular activities, STL provides a well-rounded educational experience for all students.

Student Government Social Media

Below is a list of some of the clubs offered at St. Theresa Of Lisieux Catholic High School. Each club has a link to their social media page where you can learn more about their activities:

  • Athletic Council

    The Athletic Council organizes sports events and activities to encourage school spirit and student involvement in athletics.

  • Chaplaincy Council

    The Chaplaincy Council coordinates spiritual activities, including masses, retreats, and community service events.

  • Cub Council

    The Cub Council organizes fun activities and fundraisers to promote a positive and inclusive school culture.

  • Diversity and Equity Council

    The Diversity and Equity Council advocates for inclusivity and organizes events celebrating different cultures and identities.

  • Environmental Council

    The Environmental Council promotes sustainability initiatives and organizes events like cleanups and eco-awareness campaigns.

  • Intramural Council

    The Intramural Council organizes intramural sports leagues, ensuring all students have the opportunity to participate in sports regardless of skill level.

  • Social Justice Council

    The Social Justice Council works on projects that advocate for social issues and organizes awareness campaigns and charity events.

  • Spirit Council

    The Spirit Council plans and executes events that aim to build school spirit and create a lively atmosphere for students.

  • Student Council

    The Student Council represents the student body, organizing events, improving school policies, and voicing student concerns to the administration.
